Help and support building justice sector software

Get help at any point in your project, to make sure your integration with our APIs goes smoothly.

For urgent court or hearing enquiries
This page is for teams building or integrating software with HMCTS APIs. If you need help with a live court case, hearing, or other urgent non-technical matter, contact your local court or tribunal directly — this Marketplace team cannot assist with individual case enquiries.

Live incidents and service updates

If you already have production access (following step 6 of our onboarding process — get your access approved), you can receive service updates and raise live incidents affecting an API you consume.

To raise a live incident, contact the HMCTS Digital and Technology Solutions service desk. Do not contact them before you have completed onboarding and been issued production credentials.

Live incidents and service status updates are for production issues only. For general questions or help getting started, use the contact form below instead.

Help and support connecting to our APIs

For help and support connecting to our APIs, or to get started with onboarding, use the contact form. We aim to respond within 2 working days.

To help us find the person best placed to support you, please let us know:

  • who you are and what you do
  • what organisation you represent
  • what you are trying to achieve
  • which APIs you are using or interested in
